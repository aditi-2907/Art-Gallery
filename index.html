ABOUT US!

The Illuminati Gallery is a fast growing online art gallery, connecting people with art and artists they love.
We offer a beautiful selection of paintings, drawings, sculpture, design, figurative, illustrative art
and photography in various price ranges. We provide a platform for artists around the world with an
expertly curated environment in which to exhibit and sell their work. in addition to this,
for a very small fee artists may exhibit an art selection of their choice, with the possibility
to communicate directly with art lovers. If the artist wishes, it is possible to build a larger
profile with links to his/her own website. Our aim is to connect art lovers directly with the artists.
